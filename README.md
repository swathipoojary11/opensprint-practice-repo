1. Introduction
The Open Sprint Workshop is an interactive session designed to introduce students to the concept of open collaboration, innovation, and rapid problem solving. The workshop brings together participants to learn new technologies, share ideas, and work on creative solutions in a short period of time. It encourages teamwork, learning, and practical implementation of ideas.

2. Objectives
The main objective of the Open Sprint Workshop is to help participants understand how to work in a collaborative environment and build solutions efficiently. During the workshop, students explore different tools, technologies, and development practices. It also helps improve problem-solving skills, creativity, and communication among participants.

3. Outcome
By the end of the Open Sprint Workshop, participants gain hands-on experience in developing ideas and working as a team. They learn how to quickly plan, design, and implement solutions within a limited time. The workshop also motivates students to continue learning new technologies and contribute to innovative projects in the future.
